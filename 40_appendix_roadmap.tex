\section{Appendix Roadmap}
\label{app:roadmap}

The appendix keeps secondary theoretical and implementation material out of the
main method story.
It first states limitations and extended related work, then preserves the full
formal model, the longer refinement and DGM development, implementation
extensions, the projection-memory bridge, and formal proofs.
\begin{itemize}
\item {\bf Part 1.} Appendix~\ref{app:limitations} clarifies what the theory proves and what it leaves to modeling, statistics, and experiments.
\item {\bf Part 2.} Appendix~\ref{app:related} gives the extended related-work discussion.
\item {\bf Part 3.} Appendices~\ref{sec:preliminaries}--\ref{sec:algorithms} retain the full model, value identities, and DGM formalism.
\item {\bf Part 4.} Appendix~\ref{sec:large-model-training} gives DGM-Embedding as an architectural extension; Appendix~\ref{app:dgm-extensions} collects tree special cases, data-structure costs, the optional adapter variant, systems notes, and closed-form local repair.
\item {\bf Part 5.} Appendix~\ref{sec:projection-bridge} gives the Projection Memory and CPM bridge.
\item {\bf Part 6.} Appendices~\ref{app:refinement-proofs}, \ref{app:value-proofs}, and \ref{app:algorithm-bridge-proofs} give the formal proofs.
\end{itemize}
