\section{Appendix Roadmap}
\label{app:roadmap}

The appendix keeps secondary material out of the main method story. It first states limitations, then gives extended related work, implementation extensions, additional computational checks, proof sketches, the projection-memory bridge, and formal proofs.
\begin{itemize}
\item {\bf Part 1.} Appendix~\ref{app:limitations} clarifies what the theory proves and what it leaves to modeling, statistics, and experiments.
\item {\bf Part 2.} Appendix~\ref{app:related} gives the extended related-work discussion.
\item {\bf Part 3.} Appendix~\ref{app:dgm-extensions} collects tree special cases, data-structure costs, DGM-Embedding training modes, the optional adapter variant, systems notes, and closed-form local repair.
\item {\bf Part 4.} Appendix~\ref{app:additional-checks} reports secondary computational checks.
\item {\bf Part 5.} Appendix~\ref{sec:value} gives value-of-distinction identities for standard losses.
\item {\bf Part 6.} Appendix~\ref{sec:technical-overview} gives proof sketches, and Appendix~\ref{sec:projection-bridge} gives the Projection Memory and CPM bridge.
\item {\bf Part 7.} Appendices~\ref{app:refinement-proofs}, \ref{app:value-proofs}, and \ref{app:algorithm-bridge-proofs} give the formal proofs.
\end{itemize}
