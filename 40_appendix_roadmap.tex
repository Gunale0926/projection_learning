\section{Appendix Roadmap}
\label{app:roadmap}

The appendix follows the same story as the main text. It first states the limitations of the refinement view, then situates the paper relative to prior work, and then gives formal statements and proofs.
\begin{itemize}
\item {\bf Part 1.} Appendix~\ref{app:limitations} clarifies what the theory proves and what it leaves to modeling, statistics, and experiments.
\item {\bf Part 2.} Appendix~\ref{app:related} places the paper relative to prototype methods, decision trees, information theory, sigma-algebra refinement, backpropagation, projection learning, and test-time adaptation.
\item {\bf Part 3.} Appendix~\ref{app:refinement-proofs} proves the minimal refinement and contradiction theorems.
\item {\bf Part 4.} Appendix~\ref{app:value-proofs} proves monotonicity of Bayes risk and the exact log-loss and squared-loss refinement identities.
\item {\bf Part 5.} Appendix~\ref{app:algorithm-bridge-proofs} proves the algorithmic and bridge guarantees: prototype edge separation, DGM data-structure complexity, DGM layer cost, and the bridge between partition refinement and CPM conflict.
\end{itemize}
