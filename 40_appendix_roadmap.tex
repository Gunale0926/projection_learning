\section{Appendix Roadmap}
\label{app:roadmap}

The appendix is organized to mirror the logic of the paper while giving full formal details.
\begin{itemize}
\item {\bf Part 1.} Appendix~\ref{app:limitations} states the limitations of the framework and clarifies what is not proved.
\item {\bf Part 2.} Appendix~\ref{app:related} gives related work after the technical story, so the main text can stay focused on definitions and results.
\item {\bf Part 3.} Appendix~\ref{app:projection-proofs} proves the projection-learning facts: the Bregman identity, the projection characterization theorem, soft projection bounds, KL projection, and the gradient-descent relation.
\item {\bf Part 4.} Appendix~\ref{app:memory-proofs} proves the exact memory theory: feasibility, capacity, innovation decomposition, conflict impossibility, and hard and soft conservative updates.
\item {\bf Part 5.} Appendix~\ref{app:ridge-proofs} proves the ridge-recursive and Bayesian forms of projection memory.
\end{itemize}
